# NotPogMC - Privacy Policy
_Last Updated: 17 June 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how NotPogMC ("the Bot") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By adding the Bot to your server or interacting with its commands, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information Collected
To provide gameplay features and maintain leaderboards, the Bot collects minimal, publicly available data from the Discord API. We do not collect real-life personal information (such as your legal name, email address, or physical location).

The data we collect includes:
* Discord User IDs: To associate game progress, inventory, and stats with your specific Discord account.
* Discord Server (Guild) IDs: To enable server-specific configuration, features, and local leaderboards.
* Channel IDs: To properly direct command responses and game outputs.
* User-Generated Input: Text explicitly provided by you when executing commands.

2. How We Use the Data
The data collected is used strictly for the operational functionality of the Bot. Specifically, it is used to:
* Save and retrieve your in-game progress and inventory.
* Generate global and server-wide leaderboards.
* Debug technical issues, prevent exploits, and optimize Bot performance.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We value your privacy.
* No Sale of Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties or advertising networks.
* Service Providers: Data is only processed through our secure database infrastructure solely for running the Bot.
* Legal Compliance: We will only disclose information if required by law or to comply with Discord's official Terms of Service.

4. Data Security
We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your stored data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Deletion and User Rights
You retain full control over your data. If you wish to have your data permanently deleted from our database:
* You may submit a request for data deletion in our Discord support server.
* You may also use the Bot's `/profile delete` command to wipe your profile instantly.

Removing the Bot from a server automatically halts data collection for that server, though existing user game profiles remain intact unless a deletion request is made.

6. Changes to This Policy
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as the Bot evolves. Users will be notified of any significant changes via the Bot’s official support channels or update logs. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
